Man-Fai Leung received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from the City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2019. He is currently with the School of Computing and Information Science, Faculty of Science and Engineering, Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge, U.K. His current research interests include intelligent systems, optimization, computational intelligence, and their applications. He serves as an Associate Editor for Complex & Intelligent Systems and Intelligent Systems with Applications.
